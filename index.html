Welcome
to
Wizarding World!

The Harry Potter series, created by J.K. Rowling, is a globally beloved collection of books and films that has captivated audiences of all ages. The story follows a young wizard, Harry Potter, who discovers his magical heritage on his eleventh birthday and embarks on a journey of adventure, friendship, and self-discovery at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Alongside his friends, Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, Harry faces challenges, uncovers hidden truths, and confronts the dark forces that threaten the wizarding world.

The series has expanded into a rich universe, encompassing not only the original seven books but also films, spin-offs, theme parks, and a wide array of fan creations. The magic of Harry Potter continues to inspire readers and viewers, making it one of the most influential and enduring literary and cinematic franchises in modern history.
